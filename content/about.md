# Hey, I'm Tolic 👋

I'm a Staff Software Engineer with 20+ years of experience building systems that scale. I think in patterns, optimize for maintainability, and believe the best architecture is the one that can evolve gracefully when assumptions change.

## What I Do

Currently a Staff Engineer, and I focus on:
- **System reliability and observability** - Building robust metrics, logging, and monitoring that actually help debug problems
- **Cross-team architecture** - Leading initiatives like GraphQL Gateway adoption that simplify how services communicate
- **Performance optimization** - Making background jobs faster, ETL pipelines more reliable, and systems more resilient under load

I've spent my career in high-growth companies (StackAdapt, Instacart, CultureAmp, Redbubble, Seek) solving problems like data migrations, system integrations, anti-spam automation, and event-driven architecture transitions.

## My Engineering Philosophy

**Good abstraction is hard.** Wrong abstractions are more expensive than code duplication. I've learned to focus on behavior over taxonomies and composition over inheritance.

**Legacy code isn't old code—it's code where the reasoning has been lost.** I document decisions, not just implementations, because understanding *why* matters as much as understanding *what*.

**The best management is invisible.** Like a good referee, great engineering leadership removes blockers before they become problems and creates environments where teams can do their best work.

## Current Obsessions

**AI-Native Architecture**: Building systems designed for intelligent agents, not just human developers. I'm exploring how LLMs can own modules, make decisions within constraints, and create self-evolving software.

**Event-Driven Everything**: Moving from batch processing to event streaming. From thinking in snapshots to thinking in flows. From pull-based to push-based architectures.

**Neurodivergent-Friendly Systems**: As someone with ADHD, I'm passionate about creating tools and workflows that amplify rather than constrain different thinking patterns. Checklists as external memory, automation that reduces cognitive load, and systems that work with your brain, not against it.

## What I'm Building

**AI Accountability Bot**: A GPT-powered Telegram bot that helps neurodivergent users stay focused on priorities. It integrates with Google Sheets, sends automated check-ins, and provides daily summaries. Built specifically for ADHD/ASD executive function challenges.

**Content Automation Stack**: An AI agent system that generates, scripts, narrates, and publishes psychology and relationship content across multiple platforms. Fully automated pipeline from idea to published video.

**YouTube Shorts SaaS**: Rails-based tool that automatically slices long-form YouTube videos into shorts, with upload processing and export pipeline for content creators.
